## About

Easily generate a fully customizable cookie banner, obtain consent and implement prior blocking with asynchronous reactivation.

## Functionalities

- Create your privacy and cookie policy in minutes;
- Manage consent preferences for the ePrivacy, GDPR, and CCPA. Integrated with the IAB TCF and US Privacy Framework;
- Create your terms and conditions in minutes;
- Collect GDPR consent, document opt-ins and CCPA opt-outs via your web forms;
- Document all the data processing activity within your organization.

## How to set up

1. Install the app from VTEX App Store;
2. Add the Site ID and the Cookie Policy ID in the respective fields. You can find yours at Iubenda settings page;
3. Save your changes.

## Comments

- To use the service, you must have an active Iubenda account. Some features are only available for specific plans.
- Detailed installation instructions are available at https://vtex.io/docs/app/vtex.iubenda
